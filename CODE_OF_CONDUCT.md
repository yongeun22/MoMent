# Code Of Conduct

MoMent uses a simple contributor conduct policy.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussion focused on the project.
- Assume people may be volunteering limited time.
- Protect private information, exhibition content rights, and security-sensitive details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private contact information, passwords, tokens, database files, or private media.
- Ignoring content ownership or license restrictions.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; or block participants when needed to keep the project safe and useful.

Report conduct issues through the maintainer contact path already used by the repository. Maintainer confirmation required: add a dedicated private contact if the project adopts one.
