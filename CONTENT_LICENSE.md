## Creating A New Exhibition

If you fork MoMent for another exhibition, replace the bundled photos, music, logos, QR images, and exhibition-specific text with content that you own or have permission to use.

Do not assume that files in `dist/` are reusable just because they were generated by the static exporter. Generated files may contain copies of protected exhibition content.
